Help Center

Getting Started

How do I create an account?

Click on the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner and follow the on-screen instructions.

How do I place a bid?

To place a bid, you must be logged in. Navigate to an auction item, enter your bid amount, and click "Place Bid." Ensure your balance is sufficient to cover the bid amount.

What happens if I'm outbid?

If another user places a higher bid, your previous bid amount is immediately refunded to your account balance. You will receive a notification and can choose to place a new, higher bid.

How can I deposit money?

Go to the "Deposit" page from the main navigation menu. Enter the amount you wish to deposit and select your preferred payment method (UPI, Card, or Net Banking) to complete the transaction.

Technical Support

My balance isn't updating. What should I do?

If your balance doesn't reflect a recent transaction, please try refreshing the page. If the issue persists, contact our support team with your transaction details.

I can't log in. What's wrong?

First, double-check your username and password for typos. If you still can't log in, you may need to reset your password. If the problem continues, our support team can assist you.

Still need help?

Contact our support team 24/7 through the Contact Us form.
